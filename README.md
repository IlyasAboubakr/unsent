# Unsent Messages

A place for the things you never said.

**[unsentt.vercel.app](https://unsentt.vercel.app)**

---

## What it is

Unsent Messages is an anonymous space where people drop the words they never got to say — to someone they miss, someone they love, someone they're angry at, or just to themselves. No accounts, no names. Just the message.

It started out of curiosity. I wanted to see what people carry around but never say out loud. The result is a quiet, scrollable stream of raw, unfiltered thoughts from strangers.

## How it works

Anyone can post a message anonymously. Posts show up instantly in the feed for everyone to read. There are no likes, no comments, no profiles — just words. A rate limiter keeps things from getting noisy.

## Tech

- **Frontend** — React, deployed on Vercel
- **Backend** — Node.js + Express, serverless on Vercel
- **Database** — MongoDB Atlas
